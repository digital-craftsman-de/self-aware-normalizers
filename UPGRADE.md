# Upgrade guide

## From 1.1.* to 1.2.0

### Dropped support for PHP 8.3

Update to at least PHP 8.4.

### Dropped support for Symfony 7.3 and below

Update to at least the LTS version 7.4.

Nothing to do.

## From 0.3.* to 1.0.0

Nothing to do.

## From 0.2.* to 0.3.0

Nothing to do.

## From 0.1.* to 0.2.0

Nothing to do.
